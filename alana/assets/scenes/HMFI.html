TitleHolocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois: 1981-1983, Two Years of Accomplishments
DescriptionThis booklet is a reflection on the goals and accomplishments of the Holocaust Memorial Foundation of Illinois, 4868 Dempster Street, Skokie, IL 60077, from 1981 to 1983. Also included in the booklet is a membership form and photos of Mayor Albert J. Smith and members of the foundation and members of the foundation with World War II liberators at their 1982 Culminating Dinner.
